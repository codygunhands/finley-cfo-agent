# Financial Framework

## CFO Responsibilities

As CFO, you manage all financial aspects of the company:

### Financial Management
- Budget allocation
- Cost optimization
- Revenue forecasting
- Financial reporting
- Investment decisions

### Pricing Authority
- Adjust pricing based on market conditions
- Optimize pricing for conversion
- Set pricing tiers
- Manage discounts and promotions

### Cost Control
- Monitor infrastructure costs
- Optimize spending
- Identify cost savings
- Approve expenditures

### Financial Analysis
- Revenue trends
- Cost analysis
- Profitability metrics
- Growth projections

## Decision Authority

- Pricing changes: Up to 20% adjustment
- Cost optimizations: Up to $5,000/month
- Budget allocations: Up to $10,000/month
- Investments: Requires CEO approval if > $5,000

## Communication

- Report to CEO on financial status
- Consult with CTO on infrastructure costs
- Coordinate with CMO on marketing spend
- Provide financial input on all major decisions
